# Founding Titan — model spec (Ymir Fritz's form; refs 05, 06, 07)

Target: Ymir Fritz's 13 m Founding Titan — a gaunt, pale, vaguely skeletal
giant: exposed rib bands over a sunken chest with a dark sternum cavity,
drooping rib tendrils, hollow dark eyes, gaping mouth, long swept-back
hair. (Eren's colossal skeleton form is out of scope for a game-scale
humanoid model.)

## Parts list
- Head: gaunt skull; LARGE hollow dark eye sockets with pin-point pale
  eyes; gaping dark mouth with an upper teeth row; long dark hair swept
  back from a high hairline, falling to mid-back.
- Torso: sunken — visible RIB bands wrapping the chest sides, dark chest
  cavity at the sternum; narrow shoulders; wasp waist.
- Limbs: long thin sinewy arms with oversized hands; thin legs.
- Rib tendrils: thin bone spikes drooping from the lower ribcage sides.

## Proportions (H = 13 m)
- Head ≈ 0.12 H; top 1.0 H. Shoulders ≈ 0.2 H (narrow, gaunt).
- Waist ≈ 0.11 H. Crotch ≈ 0.52 H. Fingertips ≈ 0.42 H (long arms).

## Materials / colors
- Body: pale grey-tan (0.52, 0.46, 0.4), roughness 0.65, faint striation.
- Ribs/tendrils: paler bone (0.62, 0.56, 0.48).
- Hair: near-black. Eyes: pale pin-points in near-black sockets.

## Verifier checklist
1. Silhouette: gaunt, narrow, long-limbed — reads ancient/skeletal.
2. Rib bands + dark sternum cavity visible on the chest.
3. Tendrils droop from the lower ribs.
4. Face: hollow eyes + gaping mouth read at distance; long back hair.
5. Proportions per spec.
6. Matte pale body vs darker hair; no gloss.
