# War Hammer Titan — model spec (from refs 01, 03, 08)

Target: Lara Tybur's 15 m War Hammer Titan — a slender marble-white giant
wrapped in fibrous hardened flesh, blank mask face, striped muzzle/neck,
carrying the giant spiked war hammer.

## Parts list
- Head: smooth bald white dome; dark horizontal eye slits (no eyeballs);
  the lower face is a red/white VERTICALLY striped band (the "teeth
  curtain") flowing into red/white ringed neck wraps.
- Body: lean athletic, entirely white/grey fibrous muscle — fiber
  striation everywhere except the smooth head dome.
- Hammer: long white pole held in the right hand, massive cross-hatched
  head with a grid of spikes, resting head-down.

## Proportions (H = 15 m)
- Head ≈ 0.12 H; top 1.0 H. Slender: shoulders ≈ 0.24 H, waist ≈ 0.13 H.
- Crotch ≈ 0.52 H; long legs; fingertips ≈ 0.42 H.
- Hammer: pole ≈ 0.6 H long; head ≈ 0.12 H cube-ish.

## Materials / colors
- Body: white-grey (0.72, 0.72, 0.7), roughness 0.6, fiber striation with
  grey dip.
- Stripes/rings: deep red (0.45, 0.1, 0.08) on white.
- Eye slits: near-black. Hammer: same white-grey, spikes bone white.

## Verifier checklist
1. Silhouette: slender WHITE titan — reads marble statue, not flesh.
2. Face: blank dome + dark slits + striped lower face; NO hair.
3. Neck: red/white rings under the striped chin band.
4. Hammer present in right hand with spiked head.
5. Fiber striation on body, smooth dome head.
6. Matte throughout.
