# Attack Titan — model spec (from refs 01, 02, 03, 09, 10)

Target: Eren's 15 m Attack Titan — an athletic skinless-muscle humanoid with
long black hair, pointed ears, and the skull grin. Whole body is tan/
terracotta exposed muscle with visible fiber direction.

## Parts list
- Head: skull-like face; permanent lipless grin exposing a full teeth band;
  sunken cheeks; strong brow ridge; green deep-set eyes; prominent nose;
  POINTED ears; long shaggy black hair (center part, reaches shoulders).
- Torso: athletic V-taper — broad shoulders, defined pecs, tight waist,
  visible ab structure. All muscle, no skin, no plates.
- Arms: long and muscular, fingertips reach mid-thigh; defined biceps and
  forearms; big hands with fingers.
- Legs: long; quads and calves clearly bulge; bare muscular feet with toes.
- Color: tan/terracotta muscle everywhere (NOT red like Armored's gaps).

## Proportions (fraction of H = 15 m, athletic ~7.5-head build)
- Head ≈ 0.13 H incl jaw; top of head 1.0 H; chin ≈ 0.885 H.
- Shoulder span ≈ 0.27 H; waist width ≈ 0.14 H (strong taper).
- Hip line ≈ 0.52 H; legs long, knee ≈ 0.29 H.
- Fingertips ≈ 0.42 H (mid-thigh).

## Materials / colors
- Muscle: tan/terracotta (0.55, 0.33, 0.2), roughness 0.6, strong fiber
  striation bump following the length of limbs.
- Hair: near-black (0.03, 0.025, 0.02), long strands + geometry cap.
- Teeth: bone white. Eyes: green-grey, faint glow, deep-set.

## Verifier checklist
1. Silhouette: lean athletic humanoid — NOT bulky; V-taper torso obvious.
2. Grin: wide white teeth band always visible, no lips.
3. Hair: long, black, shaggy, reaches shoulders; ears pointed.
4. Muscle-fiber read on major groups (pecs, quads, biceps).
5. Proportions: fingertips mid-thigh, legs long, head ~1/7.5 of height.
6. Color: uniform tan muscle body; only hair/teeth/eyes differ.
