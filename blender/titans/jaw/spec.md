# Jaw Titan — model spec (Porco Galliard version; refs 01, 03, 10, 11)

Target: the 5 m Jaw Titan — smallest of the nine. Pale-skinned athletic
body, white bony skull-muzzle mask with jagged teeth, huge blonde mane,
white claws on hands and feet.

## Parts list
- Head: white bone mask covering nose-to-chin with a jagged lipless teeth
  arc; red-rimmed eyes; huge blonde mane sweeping back from the face and
  down to the chest, covering the ears.
- Body: pale/tan SKIN (not exposed muscle), lean and athletic; slightly
  long arms for the lunging crouch.
- Claws: long white bone claws on fingers and toes; red exposed-muscle
  patches at the wrists/backs of hands and at the ankles/feet.

## Proportions (H = 5 m)
- Head ≈ 0.14 H incl mane; top 1.0 H.
- Shoulder span ≈ 0.26 H; lean torso; crotch ≈ 0.53 H.
- Fingertips (claw tips) ≈ 0.4 H.

## Materials / colors
- Skin: pale tan (0.55, 0.38, 0.28), roughness 0.65, subtle bump only (no
  fiber striation — this titan has skin).
- Bone (mask + claws): off-white (0.85, 0.82, 0.75), roughness 0.4.
- Mane: warm blonde (0.75, 0.62, 0.3). Eyes: amber, red-rimmed sockets.

## Verifier checklist
1. Silhouette: SMALL lean titan with a huge maned head and clawed limbs.
2. White skull-muzzle mask with jagged teeth reads at distance.
3. Mane: blonde, back-swept, reaches the chest.
4. Claws visible on hands + feet; red muscle patches at wrists/ankles.
5. Body is smooth pale skin (no fiber texture).
6. Matte materials; the bone mask slightly glossier than skin.
