# Colossus Titan — model spec (from refs 03, 06, 07, 01)

Target: the 60 m Colossal Titan — a rangy, long-limbed giant of exposed
crimson musculature with pale sinew streaks and the skeletal ear-to-ear grin.

## Parts list
- Head: bald muscle-fiber skull; NO hair, NO lips — a huge grin of exposed
  teeth spanning the lower face; small lidless pale eyes; heavy brow ridge;
  small ears; slight nose bump only.
- Torso: massive deep ribcage, comparatively narrow waist; muscle bundles
  run in long vertical fibers.
- Limbs: extremely LONG legs (leggy silhouette, hip ≈ 0.53 H) and long arms
  with big hands; whitish sinew/tendon streaks at shins, knees, outer
  thighs, spine, forearms, achilles.
- Color: deep crimson muscle + pale pink-white sinew accents. No steam on
  the model (steam is a transient FX concern, not geometry).

## Proportions (fraction of H = 60 m)
- Head ≈ 0.09 H, tiny relative to body; top of skull = 1.0 H.
- Shoulder span ≈ 0.23 H; ribcage depth ≈ 0.2 H.
- Hip/crotch ≈ 0.53 H; knee ≈ 0.29 H — legs read VERY long.
- Fingertips ≈ 0.43 H.

## Materials / colors
- Muscle: crimson (0.42, 0.1, 0.07), roughness 0.65, strong vertical fiber
  striation.
- Sinew accents: pale flesh (0.78, 0.64, 0.55), roughness 0.5, flat strips.
- Teeth: bone white, large, individually separated. Eyes: pale, faint glow.

## Verifier checklist
1. Silhouette: rangy and leggy — long legs/arms, deep chest, small head.
2. Grin: wide ear-to-ear teeth row, no lips, reads at distance.
3. Bald fiber skull — no hair anywhere.
4. Pale sinew streaks visible at shins/knees/spine against crimson.
5. Proportions per spec (hip ≈ 0.53 H, head ≈ 0.09 H).
6. Color: crimson + pale sinew; matte, no gloss.
