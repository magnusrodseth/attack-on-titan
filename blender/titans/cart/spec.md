# Cart Titan — model spec (from refs 02, 05, 01, 03)

Target: Pieck's quadrupedal Cart Titan — ~2.8 m at the shoulder, ~7 m nose
to rump. Pinkish skin, human-like arms as forelegs (palms on the ground),
crouched hind legs, arched back, long horse-like face with big lips.

## Parts list
- Head: elongated muzzle sloping down-forward, big fleshy lips at the end,
  wide mouth slit; eyes high on the skull; visible ears; shaggy brown hair
  on the crown.
- Body: horizontal spine, arched back with a slight hump at the shoulders,
  sagging belly; lean.
- Forelegs: human arms, elbows locked straight, palms flat on the ground
  with fingers forward.
- Hind legs: crouched Z-fold — knees forward, ankles back, feet flat.
- No armament in v1 (bare version; the harness/turret is a separate prop
  for a later pass).

## Proportions
- Shoulder height ≈ 2.8 m; head top ≈ 3.1 m; body length ≈ 7 m.
- Muzzle ≈ 1.2 m long, tip at ~2.0 m height.

## Materials / colors
- Skin: mottled pink flesh (0.55, 0.35, 0.3), roughness 0.65, noise bump
  (NO fiber striation — this titan has skin).
- Lips: darker flesh (0.45, 0.28, 0.22). Hair: dark brown, shaggy.
- Eyes: dark amber.

## Verifier checklist
1. Silhouette: unmistakably QUADRUPED — arms straight down, hind legs
   folded, back arched, head thrust forward.
2. Muzzle: long with big lips; mouth line visible at game distance.
3. Hair: brown shaggy crown; ears visible.
4. Pink skin, matte, mottled — no muscle fiber.
5. Proportions per spec (long low body).
6. All four limbs firmly reach the ground plane.
