# Female Titan — model spec (from refs 01, 03, 05, 06)

Target: Annie's 14 m Female Titan — athletic feminine two-tone body: red
exposed muscle on limbs/shoulders/sides, PALE skin panels on torso front/
back, glutes, and face. Blonde bob, blue eyes, red cheek fiber patches.

## Parts list
- Head: pale skin face with red muscle fiber patches on the cheeks; blue
  eyes; subtle mouth line (has lips, unlike Attack); chin-length blonde bob
  covering the ears, side-swept fringe.
- Torso: pale chest plate with rib creases, pale center belly panel with a
  red segmented CHAIN-LINK core column, pale pelvis triangle and glutes,
  pale upper back; everything else red muscle.
- Limbs: red muscle arms and legs, long and lean; pale ankle wrap bands;
  feminine hands/feet.
- Figure: narrow shoulders (0.21 H), very tight waist (0.12 H), wider hips;
  long legs (crotch ≈ 0.52 H).

## Proportions (H = 14 m)
- Head ≈ 0.115 H incl hair; top 1.0 H.
- Shoulder span ≈ 0.21 H; waist ≈ 0.12 H; hip width ≈ 0.16 H.
- Crotch ≈ 0.52 H; fingertips ≈ 0.43 H.

## Materials / colors
- Muscle: red-pink (0.5, 0.16, 0.11), roughness 0.65, fiber striation with
  color dip.
- Pale skin: (0.72, 0.58, 0.48), roughness 0.6, SSS, light creasing.
- Hair: olive blonde (0.6, 0.55, 0.28). Eyes: blue, faint glow.

## Verifier checklist
1. Silhouette: feminine athletic — narrow shoulders, tight waist, long legs.
2. Two-tone read: pale torso panels + glutes vs red limbs, from all views.
3. Chain-link core column visible down the belly.
4. Face: pale with red cheek patches, blue eyes, blonde bob covering ears.
5. Proportions per spec.
6. Matte materials; red is muscle-fiber, not plastic.
