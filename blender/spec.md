# Beast Titan — model spec (from refs 01, 03, 05, 08)

Stylized "little demo" target: readable Beast Titan silhouette + fur/skin split, not sculpt-level detail.

## Parts list
- Head: small relative to body, elongated muzzle, pointed ears, dark beard. Bare skin face.
- Torso: very broad furred shoulders/traps forming a mane; bare skin pecs, abs, and a large round belly.
- Arms: extremely long (knuckle-dragger), fully furred; large bare-skin hands.
- Pelvis/legs: furred like shorts + legs; comparatively short legs; bare-skin feet.
- Back: fur-covered, slight hunch.

## Proportions (fraction of total height H, measured off ref 05/08)
- Total height H = 17 m (canon).
- Head height ≈ 0.10 H; head sits low between shoulders, slightly forward.
- Shoulder span ≈ 0.42 H (dominant feature).
- Arms: shoulder→fingertip ≈ 0.60 H; fingertips reach ~0.15 H above ground (mid-calf).
- Legs: hip ≈ 0.47 H; legs look short vs the torso+arms mass.
- Belly: widest bare-skin mass, centered ≈ 0.55 H.

## Materials / colors
- Fur: very dark chocolate brown, matte (roughness ~0.95), slight noise bump.
- Skin: pale tan/beige, semi-matte (roughness ~0.65).
- No gloss anywhere; refs read matte.

## Verifier checklist
1. Silhouette: arms clearly longer than legs, hands near mid-calf.
2. Shoulders much wider than hips; head small and sunk low.
3. Bare skin ONLY on: face, pecs/abs/belly, hands, feet. Everything else fur-dark.
4. Belly reads as a big round mass; pecs distinct above it.
5. Beard present under chin; ears visible in front view.
6. Colors: dark brown fur vs tan skin, both matte.
